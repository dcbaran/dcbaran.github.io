I'm Denise, the creator of this website. I'm passionate about experience design, content strategy, and empathy for users above all. My mission as I gain more experience in design is to absorb as much knowledge as I can from as many human perspectives as possible in order to truly do some good with what I create.

I recently began learning graphic design techniques, and I've been collecting my favorite projects from my class this semester into a gallery. I like to look for volunteer opportunities to create graphic designs so that I can build my skills. Most recently, I designed a logo for the Native American Student Association.
